Verdict

The codebase responded to the v1 report the way a healthy engineering org should: it converted findings into tickets and shipped them. Quality rose 62 → 83 and architecture 3.2 → 3.9 in eight days, with the two defining v1 weaknesses — no auth, no observability — genuinely resolved.

The honest caveat: the score would be ~88 if the new infrastructure were finished. One CRITICAL (empty default JWT key) and four HIGH edges (WebSocket auth, red tsc, worker-metrics scrape gap, missing CSRF) are holding it back — plus the v1 items that never got a ticket (automated backups, Docker socket). Close the one-week "Immediate" list and MarketHawk is credibly production-grade.
